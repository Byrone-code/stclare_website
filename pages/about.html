About St.Clare Girls Centre

Founded in 2014, St.Clare has been a cornerstone of quality secondary education, nurturing generations of leaders, innovators, and community contributors.

Our Mission & Values

We believe every student deserves the best opportunity to succeed

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, sports, arts, and character development.

Integrity

We foster honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct in all aspects of school life.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity and ensure every student feels valued, safe, and supported.

Service

We encourage a spirit of giving back to the community, locally and globally.
