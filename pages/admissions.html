Admissions

Join the St.Clare family. We welcome Form 1 and transfer students each year. Here is everything you need to know about applying.

Application Process

Follow these steps to secure your place at St.Clare

October – November 2026

Submit Application Form

Download and complete the application form. Submit along with all required documents to the admissions office or via email.

December 2026

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants sit a written assessment covering Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge.

January 2027

Admission Offers Sent

Successful candidates receive offer letters by post and email. Acceptance must be confirmed within 14 days.

February 2027

Enrolment & Fees

Complete enrolment forms and pay the first-term fees to secure your child's place. Bursary applications open at this stage.

Requirements

Prepare the following before applying

Documents Needed

  • ✓ Birth certificate (copy)
  • ✓ KCPE result slip
  • ✓ 2 passport photos
  • ✓ Previous school report
  • ✓ Parent/Guardian ID

Fees Structure (2027)

  • ✓ Tuition: Free/term
  • ✓ Activity fee: Free
  • ✓ ICT levy: Free
  • ✓ Boarding: Free
  • ✓ Bursaries available

Entry Requirements

  • ✓ Min KCPE score: 300
  • ✓ Good conduct certificate
  • ✓ Pass entrance assessment
  • ✓ Interview (for transfers)
